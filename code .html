Behind the Scenes at Zara Supply Chain

Zara was found in Spain in 1974 and is the largest company in the Inditex group. The company specializes in fast fashion and products such as clothing, accessories, swimwear, shoes and perfumes. Focus on fast fashion, Zara changes its clothing designs frequently every two weeks on average comparison with two or three months from competitors. Each month, around 1000 distinct designs are distributed to its stores in worldwide while its competitors only carry 200 to 400 designs each month. The company focuses on women from 24–35 years old. To order to attract their customers, Zara stores are located in town centers and place with high concentrations of women in that range of age. Furthermore, short-supply of products gives their customers a sense of urgency and reason to buy products before run out of stock. In addition, Zara prices products based on demand instead of producing cost. As a result, Zara does not have lots of excess inventory nor does it need to do huge discount to clear its items.

ザラは1974年にスペインに創立され、現在Inditex グループの最大の会社です。ファストファッションに注目され、衣服、アクセサリ、水着、靴と香水の製品を製造しています。ファストファッションを注目するため、平均して２週間ごとに衣服のデザインを頻度に変更し、他社の２、3カ月と比較してます。毎月、約1000異なるデザインを世界中の店舗に配達されますが、他社は200~400デザインしか持ってません。同社の顧客のターゲットは24から35歳の女性です。顧客を引き付ける為、ザラの店舗は町の中心場所、この年齢の女性が集中してる場所に設置されます。さらに、製品の供給不足の目的は顧客に売れ切る前に製品を購入する緊急感と購入理由を作ります。又、価格の設定は生産コストに関わらず需要に基づいてます。その結果は過剰な在庫がなく、アイテムをクリアするため、大幅の割引を行うのも必要ありません。

The heart of the company and its supply chain is huge, highly automated distribution center called "The cube". Agents of company are always looking for new fashion trends at clubs, social gatherings. After they see inspiring designs, the design sketches will be sent to the garment designers at the Cube. After products are designed, they take around 10- 14 days to be ready in the stores. From the first step until finished good in stores, it can take 4–5 weeks. If a design does not sell well within a week, it will be withdrawn from shops and be replaced by new designs. However, Zara also has a range of regular designs that carried over from year to year. Customers visit Zara stores 17 times per year on average compared to 3 times per year for competitors. The company tries to keep its stock fresh. Zara has a highly stock turnover, it is 12 inventory turns per year compared to 3–4 per year for competitors. Each week, stores place orders twice and this will be used of planning in factory. Based on real and short-term data makes forecasts very accurate more than competitors who have longer order time.

会社とそのサプライチェンの中心は「キューブ」と呼ばれる巨大で高度に自動化された流通センターです。会社のエージェントはクラブや社交の集まりで新しいファッションのトレンドを探しています。素晴らしいデザインを見るとデザインスケッチをデザイン部署に送られます。デザイン完了されるから店舗まで整うのは10～14日掛かります。最初からのステップから製品の完成まで4–5週間かかります。デザインは一週間以内に売れなくなると店舗で撤回されて、新しいデザインを置き返されます。しかし、年に関わらず、普通なデザインのアイテムも販売します。顧客は平均で年に１７回ザラの店舗を訪れますが、他社にこの率は3回しかありません。ザラの在庫回転率が高く、一年間１２回になりますが、他社の率は３－４回しかないです。毎週、店舗は2回注文し、このデータを基づいて、工場で計画を立ち、生産を行います。実際と短期なデータを基づいて計画正確になります。

Zara only buy some types of fabric with large quantities but can change each year. The process of design, related to cutting and dyeing are made in-house. The company buys raw fabric from suppliers in Italy, Spain, Portugal and Greece. By this, raw material suppliers can supply and deliver quickly to The Cube. In average, those suppliers deliver within 5 days after orders are placed by truck. While some of competitors are using outsource in Asia, most of Zara's products are manufactured in Spain, Portugal, Turkey and Morocco at a dozen company-owned factories (50% in Spain, 26% in the rest of Europe, 24% in Asia and Africa). Zara uses low cost and cheap labor, around 3000 workers in manufacturing operation in Spain with an average wage of 8.00 euros per hour and others in Asian with 0.4 euros per hour.

ザラは大量の一部タイプのファブリックしかを購入しませんが毎年変更することができます。デザインや裁断と染色に関するプロセスは社内で行われます。イタリア、スペイン、ポルトガス、ギリキャから生地を購入しています。これにより、キューブに素材は早めに提供と配信できます。平均で注文後5日以内にトラックで配達されます。他社はアジアでアウトソースを使用していますが、ザラの製品は殆んど社内でスペイン、ポルトガス、トルコ、モロッコに生産しています（スペインに50%、ヨーロッパ26%、アジアとアフリカ 24%）。3000人の労働者は工場に作業されて、スペインで一時当たり平均8.00ユーロ、アジアで0.4ユーロ相当です。

After making, clothing is process at The Cube through inspecting, sorting, tagging, and loading process. Zara can deliver completed product to stores worldwide in a few days: China-48 hours, Europe- 24 hours, Japan -72 hours, US- 48hours. Trucks are using to deliver within Europe and air freight is used to ship to other market. Zara can afford manufacturing in Europe instead of outsourcing in Asia (cheaper wage/hour) and air freight charge because it does not spend much money for advertisement and does not need to do much discount. Items are already on hanger with tags, price on them and already for sales when they are arrived to stores.

製造後、衣料品は検査、並べ替え、タグ付、ロードのプロセスはキューブに処理されます。ザラは数日に世界中の店舗に完成製品を配達できます；中国-48時間、ヨーロッパ-24時間,日本-72時間、US- 48時間。ヨーロッパ以内にトラックで配送しますが、他の市場に航空貨物で運送されます。広告に多くお金を費やさず、多くの割引が必要ない為、ヨーロッパでの生産と航空貨物を行う事ができます。商品はすでにタグ付きでハンガーに掛けられ、店舗に到着時点で販売できます。

In summary, the success of Zara is based on the supply chain with the distribution center "The Cube". Pricing by market demand, short lead times for manufacturing & delivery combined with unique design fashion, these all allow Zara to offer customer more styles, more choices with short-time period. The company also creates a sense of urgency to buy its products before run out of stock quickly. Zara sells 85% of its items at full price compared with 60% of this rate in industry in average. Annually, there is 10% of unsold inventory compared to industry average 17–20%. Zara does not focus on online sales because items change quickly and customers are hard to see them clearly on internet. By does not spend much money on advertising, Zara uses its money to open stores to attract its customers visit frequently. Furthermore, a flexible business model allows Zara to expand its store quickly by owned, franchised or co-owned with partners. In a market with high change and hard to predict likes fashion industry, the real-time data and fast supply allow Zara to catch up with fashion trend quickly. In other words, around half of clothing sold, most of them are sold with high margin. Furthermore, Zara can increase and decrease production quickly in its own factory and because less inventory lead to less money in inventory. This model of business tracks closely to real customer demand and catches up with market status to prevent loss in prospect sales (market up) or loss by unsold items (market down).

つまり、ザラの成功はキューブに基づいています。需要に基づく価格、ショートな製造リードタイムと組み合わせる結果は短期間で多くなデザインを提供できます。さらに、在庫切れになる前に製品を購入するという緊急感を生み出してます。ザラは85%の製品を正規価格で販売してますが業界平均では60%です。毎年、売れ残り在庫は１０％ですが、業界平均は17–20％です。ザラはオンラインセールスに注力していません。なぜなら、アイテムは急速に変化し、顧客がインタネット上ではっきり製品を見るのが難しいです。他社に比べて、投資金額は広告宣伝に費やさず、新しい店舗を開き、顧客が頻繁に訪れるようにします。さらに、柔軟なビジネスモデルにより、所有やフランチャイズやパートナーと共同所有によって店舗を迅速に拡大しています。又、販売された衣類に約半分が高い利益率で販売されています。在庫が少なくなると在庫の費用が減る為、生産は迅速に調整できます。このビジネスモデルは実際の顧客の需要に密接に追跡し、市場の状況に追いつき、見込の販売の損失（加増）又売れ残り商品による損失（減速）を防げます。

This model is difficult to copy because of some reasons. In order to keep it run smoothly, the supply chain needs to be vertical integration strictly. Supply chain planners and managers need to watch customers and market to make adjustment quickly in manufacturing and operations. Economics of scale is keeping manufacturing cost low and competitive. Supply chains are composed of people, process, and technology. Technology should be used appropriate, people must be trained well, process must be put in place that enable people to apply their skill, their training and combine with technology to have best effect. Zara is using its 30 years to build and improve its real- time supply chain and training its people. This model is difficult to copy, has a lot of learning and it works well so far.

さらに、このモデルのコピーが難しいです。順調に稼働出来るため、サプライチェンが厳密に垂直総合するのが必要です。サプライチェンプランナーとマネージャは、製造と運用を調整するため、顧客と市場の状況を監視することが必要です。規模の経済性により、製造コストは低く抑え、競争力を維持しています。サプライチェンは、人、プロセス及び技術を併せて構成されます。つまり、最良な効果を得るために、適切な技術、訓練されたスタッフ、適切なプロセスと組み合わせるととです。ザラは30年をかけてリアルタイムサプライチェンを構築し、改善し、従業員をトレーニングしています。それこそ、このモデルをコピーすることが難しいですが、これまでうまく行きます。
